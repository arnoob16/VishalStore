About Us

Welcome to Asha Steel, your number one destination for all kitchen and home essentials.
We're dedicated to giving you the very best of products with a focus on three characteristics, i.e: dependability, customer service and uniqueness.
Founded in 1979, Asha Steel has completed 40+ years of service to the people of Jamshedpur.
When we first started out, our only passion and goal was to provide the best service and quality to all our customers.

We have products from 100+ brands and provide service to customers all over Jamshedpur. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
